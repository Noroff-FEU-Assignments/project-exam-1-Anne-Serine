ABOUT

For ages, the whole "I should go back to school" idea has been doing laps in my brain, but picking WHAT to study has been like navigating a maze blindfolded. I've always been the artsy type, reveling in the joy of creating stuff out of thin air. Graphic design has been on my radar for a while, thanks to my ongoing love affair with photography.

But then, out of the blue, Frontend development waltzed into my life like a cool breeze on a hot day. It just seemed so dang exciting! So, I gathered my courage, threw caution to the wind, and applied for a spot in a Frontend development study program. Fast forward a year, and here I am, having a blast.

Learning the ropes of Frontend development has been like unlocking a treasure chest of geeky delights. Seriously, who knew coding could be this much fun? Every day brings something new, and I'm soaking it all in like a sponge at a water park. Frontend development, you're a wild ride, and I'm loving every twist and turn!
